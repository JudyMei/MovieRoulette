The Inspiration


Movie Night is one of the most common get-togethers that I have with my friends and family. However, deciding what to watch is like a dark cloud that looms over our heads. We've all been there. Everyone has different tastes and most people I know are equally as indecisive as I am, making deciding what to watch an almost impossible task. That is when I decided to create “Movie Roulette,” the web app that is saving movie night one click at a time. With this app, you can have a movie randomly generated based on predefined parameters that you can feed into the site. Happy Watching!

This product uses the TMDB API but is not endorsed or certified by TMDB.
